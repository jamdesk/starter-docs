Callouts draw attention to key information.

## Types

<Note>
Helpful context or additional information.
</Note>

<Tip>
Best practices or optimization suggestions.
</Tip>

<Warning>
Important caveats or requirements.
</Warning>

```mdx

</Note>

<Warning>
Important caveats or requirements.
</Warning>
```
